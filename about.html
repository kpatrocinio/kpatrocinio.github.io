Komusta!

I’m a Filipinx-Canadian artist based in Toronto, Canada, who’s passionate about design, travel, and most importantly, hot cheetos.

I picked up my first calligraphy pen in 2017, and I have been practicing ever since. I founded KP Lettering in 2018 and currently study Graphic Design at Humber College. Since travelling and exploring is what inspires me the most, my work is heavily inspired by organic elements and nature.​​​​​​
